How to get your first Bullshit tokens (BSH)

    All you need to do is create a ERC20 compatible wallet, get someone to send you some ether to that wallet and then grab all the BSH you need through EtherDelta!

    Here's how:

    (1). Create a wallet on https://www.myetherwallet.com/ and send some ether to it. Add the BSH token by clicking "add custom token" on the right column of the screen using the following information:

    Address: 0x91bc206f0a1ffbc399b4a20a41324ed1dad2b718

    Token symbol: BSH

    Decimals: 0

    (2). Go to : https://etherdelta.github.io/#0x91bc206f0a1ffbc399b4a20a41324ed1dad2b718-ETH. This is a ERC20 token exchange platform where BSH is listed. Import your wallet by clicking on the top right of the EtherDelta webpage

    (3). Now you need to deposit some ether from your wallet to EtherDelta. Enter the amount you wish to deposit and hit "deposit". Remember to keep enough ether in your wallet for transaction fees.

    (4). Buy your BSH tokens by selecting a row in the order book. Enter the amount of BSH you wish to buy/ ETH you're willing to spend. Hit "buy".

    (5). The block confirmation should appear in the "My Transactions" section shortly. Now all that's left to do is to enter an amount in the "Withdraw" section to send your tokens from EtherDelta to your wallet

    Congratulations, you officially have your first BSH tokens. Check your balance on MyEtherWallet and you should see your BSH tokens.

    Note: the tutorial images show a wallet who already contained 1000 BSH and 0.06 ETH in EtherDelta.
